Things to do

Water Sports at Panshet Dam

5 mins away

Enjoy thrilling water sports like boating, kayaking, and jet skiing at the iconic Panshet Dam. Perfect for adventure enthusiasts.

Boating Kayaking Jet Skiing

Torna Fort Trek

45 km away

Trek to the highest fort in Pune district, known as 'Eagle's Nest'. Conquered by Shivaji Maharaj at age 16, offering panoramic views of Sahyadris.

Historic Trek Scenic Views Adventure

Sinhagad Fort

1 hour away

Visit the historic 'Lion's Fort', famous for tales of Tanaji Malusare. Experience stunning views and rich Maratha history.

Historic Site Trekking Photography

Varasgaon Waterfall

16 km away

Perfect picnic spot with breathtaking waterfall views. Best visited during monsoon season.

Waterfall Picnic Spot Monsoon Beauty

Pabe Ghat Viewpoint

38 km away

Hidden gem offering unique views of three forts: Sinhagad, Torna, and Rajgad. Perfect for photography.

Viewpoint Photography Scenic Views

Neelkantheshwar Temple

70 km away

Ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva with religious significance. Best visited during monsoon for lush green surroundings.

Temple Nature Monsoon Views
